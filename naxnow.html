The Problem

There has been over 3000 overdose deaths in San Francisco since 2020, often it is too late by the time EMS services get there.

The Solution

Drone delivery that is faster than EMS response empowering San Franciscans to act to save lives.

Application

Mobile app

A mobile app that prioritizes functionality over flair, created for the panicked mind. Form follows function, which is why most of the landing page is occupied by a big red DISPATCH button. It's easy to use at a glance during an emergency. The goal isn't to impress with complexity, but serve with purpose.

Zipline

Zipline pioneered the world's first functional drone logistics network in Rwanda for critical, medically-sensitive supplies. This operational history, backed by strong government partnerships and funding, demonstrates their systems' reliability. Their cutting-edge second-generation drone technology (like the P2 platform) is specifically designed to safely and accurately execute autonomous deliveries in dense urban environments, transforming last-mile delivery for everyday use.
